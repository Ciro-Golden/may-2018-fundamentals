Chocolate Chip Recipe


Video Recipe for Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425F
  2. Mix the ingredients
  3. Roll the dough into balls
  4. Place on baking sheet
  5. Bake for 20 minutes
